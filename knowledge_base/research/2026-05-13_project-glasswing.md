# Research: Anthropic's Project Glasswing & Claude Mythos

## Topic
Anthropic's Claude Mythos AI found thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities in every major OS and browser. The White House blocked its expansion.

## Why Now
- Announced April 8, 2026 — still generating debate in May 2026
- White House blocked expansion April 30, 2026
- Ongoing disclosure process — thousands of CVEs being filed
- Raises fundamental questions about AI and cybersecurity

## Key Facts
1. Claude Mythos Preview is an unreleased frontier model that excels at cybersecurity
2. Found thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities in every major OS (Windows, macOS, Linux, FreeBSD, OpenBSD) and every major browser
3. Found a 27-year-old bug in OpenBSD's SACK TCP implementation (added 1998)
4. Autonomously found and exploited a 17-year-old RCE in FreeBSD (CVE-2026-4747) — root access via NFS
5. Anthropic called it "too dangerous for general release"
6. Project Glasswing launched with ~50 partner organizations
7. Partners include: AWS, Apple, Broadcom, Cisco, CrowdStrike, Google, JPMorgan Chase, Linux Foundation, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Palo Alto Networks
8. Anthropic committed $100M in Mythos usage credits + $4M to open-source security orgs
9. Anthropic asked White House to expand from 50 to 120 companies — blocked
10. White House cited security concerns and NSA losing its compute share
11. Over 1,000 critical severity vulns and thousands of high severity vulns pending disclosure
12. Anthropic's argument: defenders need a head start before similar models become broadly available

## Target Audience
Everyone — this affects anyone who uses a computer, phone, or the internet

## Competitor Coverage
- The Hacker News covered the vulnerability discovery
- The Ringer asked "Could Claude Mythos destroy the internet?"
- IEEE Spectrum covered the technical capabilities
- Forrester published "10 consequences nobody's writing about"
- Most coverage is either too technical or too sensational — gap for balanced explainer

## Suggested Angle
"The AI That's Too Dangerous to Release" — explain what Mythos found, why it matters for everyone, and the unprecedented White House intervention. Balance the "wow" with practical implications.
